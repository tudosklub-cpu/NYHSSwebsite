Founding Members

The distinguished scholars who established the New York Hungarian Scientific Society in 2010

The New York Hungarian Scientific Society was founded in 2010 by a distinguished group of Hungarian scholars, scientists, and academics working in the Greater New York Metropolitan Area. These founding members represent excellence across multiple scientific disciplines.
