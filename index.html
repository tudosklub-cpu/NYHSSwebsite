About Our Society

Connecting Hungarian scientific excellence in New York since 2010

We provide a venue for Hungarian scholars, scientists and students to gather for the promotion of science and culture in the NYC area. The Society commenced its activity as a New York nonprofit corporation organized exclusively for charitable, scientific and educational purposes. Its goals are to provide a distinctive meeting place for active participants of New York's Hungarian scientific and intellectual community in a club-like environment.

🔬

Scientific Excellence

We provide a venue for Hungarian scholars, scientists and students to gather for the promotion of science, culture and the dialog between the Hungarian and New York economic, scientific and public life.
