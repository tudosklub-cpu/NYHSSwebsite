Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member?

To become a member, use the contact form above or subscribe to our mailing list below. We'll get back to you with membership information.

Are events open to non-members?

Most of our events are open to the public, though members receive priority registration and discounted admission when applicable. Check our events page for specific event details.

What languages are used at events?

While our community is Hungarian, most events are conducted in English to accommodate our diverse membership and guests. Some social events may include Hungarian language elements.

How can I propose a lecture or event?

We welcome suggestions for lectures and events! Please email us with your proposal, including topic, speaker information, and preferred dates. Our programming committee will review your submission.
